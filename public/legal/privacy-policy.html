14. SIGNAL DATA AND INFRASTRUCTURE INDEPENDENCE

RefineX processes publicly available cloud infrastructure pricing data to generate advisory signals. RefineX does not access, monitor, or collect any data from your cloud infrastructure, servers, applications, or workloads. No cloud credentials are required to use the service. Signal confidence scores are computed using deterministic algorithms with no machine learning or probabilistic AI models. All signal generation logic is auditable through our public transparency log at refinex.io/transparency.
